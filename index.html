    Our Services

    Private Wealth Management

    We help our clients prioritise their estate planning objectives and tax planning objectives through our advisory work on Trust services. We ensure that our clients get the optimal legal structure for their estate planning objectives without compromising on other core objectives of maximising utility in the management and investment of underlying assets.

    Portfolio Management

    Our Portfolio Construction process places emphasis on security selection as we work with our clients to determine appropriate constraint factors and clearly define the parameters within which we are permitted to operate. We also deploy active strategies in search of active return for our clientele to validate our value proposition.

    Market Intelligence

    As an independent advisory firm with a proprietary desk, we are able to take in a bird’s eye view of the capital markets and its operators, which positions us to give our professional opinions on investment offerings that pass our fitness and suitability tests for our clientele.

    Research

    Our detailed investment process involves rigorous research investment analyses where our best ideas are curated in recommendations to clients. Our research provides insights and professional opinions on global and local macro economic events affecting relevant capital markets of coverage. We are also able to provide focused research coverage on specific areas of interest upon request of our Private Wealth clients requiring such service.

    Advisory on Trust

    We help our clients prioritise their estate planning objectives and tax planning objectives through our advisory work on Trust services.     Advisory on Custody

    We consult on safekeeping of assets and advise our clients on suitable capital market operators to facilitate their operational objectives.
